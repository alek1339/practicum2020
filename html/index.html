Why helmet therapy?

In many cases, a child's doctor may recommend a trial of conservative treatments including physical therapy to see if the head shape problem will correct. However, children who don't respond to physical therapy and repositioning techniques are often prescribed helmets.

Posibilities for child?

In many cases, a child's doctor may recommend a trial of conservative treatments including physical therapy to see if the head shape problem will correct without the need for a helmet. This is especially the case if the child has torticollis.
